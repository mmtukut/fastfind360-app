## PRODUCTION TIPS

### Recording the Screen Demo
1. **Pre-record the dashboard interactions** (screen recording)
2. **Speed up any loading** - use time-lapse for "processing" moments
3. **Add subtle motion graphics** (if time permits):
   - Counter ticking up to 245,254
   - Buildings "popping" onto map
   - Highlight when clicking buildings

### Audio Quality
- Record voiceover separately in quiet room
- Use USB microphone if you have one (or AirPods in quiet room)
- Match audio energy to visuals (excited when revealing 63% discrepancy)

### Visual Polish
- Use **text overlays** for key numbers:
  - "63% DISCREPANCY"
  - "₦100M UNCOLLECTED ($65K)"
  - "36 STATES IN NIGERIA"
- Keep overlays on screen for 2-3 seconds minimum (readability)

### What NOT to Do
❌ Don't use fancy transitions or effects
❌ Don't add music (distracting)
❌ Don't show yourself for more than 20 total seconds
❌ Don't read from a script robotically - sound natural
❌ Don't go over 3 minutes

### The Golden Rule
**Show, don't tell.** Every time you say "we do X," the screen should be SHOWING X happening.
